About

TEDxSIMSREE is an independently organised TED-like event by Sydenham Institute of Management Studies, Research and Entrepreneurship Education. The objective of the event is to make TEDxSIMSREE a platform where community-driven people can showcase their ideas that will lead to a change in attitudes, lives and, ultimately, the world.


As a student of the organizing Institute, I believed I could contribute in better explaining the concepts of the event and giving the audience a brief introduction of the potential speakers for the same. I also emphasized on how to use this website as a medium to connect more and more people to TEDxSIMSREE.

Challenge

The target audience mainly consisted of students, hence the website had to be compatible on mobile devices. Being maiden TED event at SIMSREE, the idea and purpose behind organizing the event was to be explained to potential audience.


We also had to plan a simple ticketing platform for audience to reserve their seats for the event. The website would also provide advertising space for the sponsors and provide links to their respective websites without hampering the user experience.

Approach

A responsive design approach was selcted for the website to be compatible on multiple devices. The modern and minimal approach to the design would help the event perfectly capture the theme of the event. For ticketing purpose simple google form verification was used along with UPI transactions on registered mobile numbers.


Front end was developed using native web development tools. Media queries are extensively used for compatability across multiple devices.

Ideation

Research and References

I researched extensively about the organization on it's Official Website. This helped me understand their design guidelines as well as other important aspects of the event. We initially started with a single page website with scrolling navigation menu.


As we developed and tested the first prototype on devices with smaller screen size, we realized the scrolling area for the user was hindering the experience because of the length of the data to be presented.


We also realized a full screen menu with multiple pages worked best on mobile screen. We incorporated our insights along with initial feedback from Organizing committee to then shift to a multiple page approach.



Design Guidelines

TEDx event is an exetended initiative by TED organization, we had to adhere to guidelines provided by the organization. The logo and the color pallette were selected accordingly.

Design

Landing Page

The Landing page consisted of all the important information on a single layout. The information was divided into subsections for better understanding. Smooth fading transitions were applied to sections to improve the user experience. Feature of countdown timer was added as suggested by organizing team, the feature was developed using JavaScript and no third party widget was used for the same.

Speaker Page

A seperate page for detailed information of speaker was designed and developed to give the target audience a better idea about the presenters.

Team Page

Finally a page where in the information regarding the organizing team members was provided to contact for more details about the event.

Final Takeaways

It was a great experience to be a part of the team, working soulely on website design and devevelopment was an extremely steep learning curve. It was an eye-opening experience that taught me a lot about being lean and knowing when and where to focus your energy and efforts.


Thanks to the organizing team, I had a really enjoyable experience at this event. I was extremely fortunate to be a part of TEDxSIMSREE and to come up with a website in a short period of time.
