About

Quantix is CRISIL's data and analytics platform with information on 25,000+ companies across 200+ industries. It is a cross segment analytics tool to drive growth in the Research business.

The product positioned as a unique offering of data, tools and analytics, was released in a modular fashion and has recieved positive response from the market.

Challenge

CRISIL identified the need to utilse their large financial data repository to develop a powerful platform to serve the emerging need of better decision making.Our team was challenged to build a web based application with different tools and modules to assist its user to execute effective financial strategies.

However, the financial data that CRISIL owns covers vast segments of data that comes in different formats. With such large quantity of data and the complex computations performed on the same using the tools provided by the application, how can we organize these resources and make something simple, informative and easy to use helping the user's find what they need ?

Solution

Quantix is an online web platform which enables users to gain insights and perform complex computations over rich financial data using multiple tools and modules. The different features of the app are orgainzed with a dashboard based platform for ease of navigation and clear representation of data.

On the technology front Angular was selected as the base framework to build the application for its capability to build Single Page application in a very accurate and maintainable way. Apart from angular Amcharts library was used for data visualization using charts and graphs.

Approach

Although our brief was to develop a web application that enables users to perform complex calculations on financial data and provide accurate results with the help of different tools, we stressed that merely focusing on the function could neither compete with the other competitor apps in the market, nor had the best interests of the app's use at heart.

To differentiate ourselves in such a competitive market, we needed to find a desirable role for the application and how it would meet the both functional and aesthetic needs of the users. We wanted to design something more meaningful.

Process

When we started the process, we elected to stick with the basic user interface design process.This process allowed us to research and explore our chosen space and better understand what our users needed and the best options for delivering a solution. As we moved forward we were able to iterate on our design and validate the direction of our project.

Overall, this process was a great means to ensuring that our project delivered a solid solution.

Ideation

Sketching

For getting started with the ideation phase, I started working on paper sketches. Our research along with the inputs provided by the stakeholders helped me understand context knowledge and because of context knowledge, I was able to produce different ideas.



Wireframing

Once we finalized on the basic layout from the sketching phase, we worked on low fidelity wireframes. We shared the wireframes across different stakeholders for their inputs and confirmation to proceed with the final design.

Dashboard
