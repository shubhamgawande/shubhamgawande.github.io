About

Quantix is CRISIL's data and analytics platform with information on 25,000+ companies across 200+ industries. It is a cross-segment analytics tool to drive growth in the Research business.


The product positioned as a unique offering of data, tools, and analytics, was released in a modular fashion and has received a positive response from the market.

Challenge

CRISIL identified the need to utilize their large financial data repository to develop a powerful platform to serve the emerging need for better decision making. Our team was challenged to build a web-based application with different tools and modules to assist its user to execute effective financial strategies.


However, the financial data that CRISIL owns covers vast segments of data that comes in different formats. With such large quantity of data and the complex computations performed on the same using the tools provided by the application, how can we organize these resources and make something simple, informative and easy to use helping the user's find what they need?

Solution

Quantix is an online web platform which enables users to gain insights and perform complex computations over rich financial data using multiple tools and modules. The different features of the app are organized with a dashboard based platform for ease of navigation and clear representation of data.


On the technology front, Angular was selected as the base framework to build the Application for its capability to build Single Page application n a very accurate and maintainable way. Apart from angular Amcharts library was used for data visualization using charts and graphs.

Approach

Although our brief was to develop a web application that enables users to perform complex calculations on financial data and provide accurate results with the help of different tools, we stressed that merely focusing on the function could neither compete with the other competitor apps in the market, nor had the best interests of the app's use at heart.


To differentiate ourselves in such a competitive market, we needed to find a desirable role for the application and how it would meet both the functional and aesthetic needs of the users. We wanted to design something more meaningful.

Process

When we started the process, we elected to stick with the basic user interface design process. This process allowed us to research and explore our chosen space and better understand what our users needed and the best options for delivering a solution. As we moved forward we were able to iterate on our design and validate the direction of our project.

Overall, this process was a great means of ensuring that our project delivered a solid solution.

Ideation

Sketching

For getting started with the ideation phase, I started working on paper sketches. Our research along with the inputs provided by the stakeholders helped me understand context knowledge and because of context knowledge, I was able to produce different ideas.



Wireframing

Once we finalized on the basic layout from the sketching phase, we worked on low fidelity wireframes. We shared the wireframes across different stakeholders for their inputs and confirmation to proceed with the final design.

Design

Dashboard

This is the first screen that the user will see after they login to the application. The goal of the UI design is to keep it quite simplistic and to provide a quick overview of different modules available in the application. It also provides the user with single-click access to the core modules.

Industry Profile

Industry Profile is one of the core modules of the application. The module provides the user with access to data of about 200+ industries across India with the ability to perform analytical operations to gain insights. Being one of the core modules of application it was also one of the most difficult ones to design considering the amount of data that was to be made available to the user. The data was represented in tabular and graphical form along with filters and other input operations whenever necessary.

Industry Benchmark

Industry Benchmark Module allows the user with the ability to generate financial reports as per the user's criteria. Depending on the input provided by the user, the report generated will be made available on the same page with the ability to download in Excel or PDF Format. The user could also save the input selection for further use which can be regenerated with the load and generate selections.

Portfolio

Similar to the Industry Profile, users have the ability to manage their credit portfolio over the application. A feature of our design to improve the micro-interactions and make the data available to the user in a compact form. Focusing on this feature allowed us to structure the information in a way that maximum information is made available to the user as quickly as possible with the limited scrollable area.

Development

Once the wireframe designs were ready and approved by the business team, we proceeded with the Front-end Development along with a team of dedicated developers and business analysts. Most of the Front-end development was done in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Additionally, Amcharts library was used to render the data in graphical form.

Final Takeaways

The Quantix Application was a very large and complex working application and took several months to design and create with multiple releases. To arrive at the final design, we completed a thorough analysis of all the sections and also with the business team to make sure all their requirements were met in terms of overall performance. Additionally, I have received positive feedback from the users about the simplified and direct approach towards the design and the complete user experience.
