Equinox - Your Potential. Our Platform.

A national level technical symposium for computer enthusiasts held at Nashik-MH, India.

My Role
  • Branding
  • Print + Merchandise Design
Client
  • Computer Department - KKWIEER
Tools
  • Adobe Photoshop

Timeline
  • March 2015

About

Equinox is a national level technical symposium held at K.K. Wagh College of Engineering, Nashik-MH. This event is one of the major event's of the institute and is conducted annually.


The main agenda of the event is to provide students with a unique platform to learn new technologies and to compete with the students across the nation in the field of I.T. and Computer Science. Multiple Seminars and Competitions are conducted during the event with over 500+ participants.

Challenge

From an event perspective, the main target audience were Computer enthusiasts across different colleges. Thus the main challenge was to generate maximum awareness and participation.


Being a student-funded event, a limited section of the budget was provided for Branding/Design and promotions. Hence a proper utilization of print-media and digital-medium was necessary. Along with that Equinox also needed to stand out from the pack as a top-tier student-driven event.

Approach

Each branding process start's with an understanding of the product/event's uniqueness and value. Because the event wanted a high-end professional look, we kept the branding very clean, minimal and modern. To achieve this a neutral color palette was established for the core branding.


The result was an eye-catching identity that helped the event portray its uniqueness and energy. We also extended the visual system to other aspects of the event, to make it more our approach more efficient.

Process

When I started this project a lot of attention was given towards gathering inputs and research from the target audience. My own experience being a student and participating in such events helped in recognizing the areas of improvement and the components that needed to be changed


I primarily worked on the logo and color palette in the beginning and then gradually worked on promotional posters and brochure. With the increasing response and the hype generated, I also worked towards designing merchandise which still has a record sale across all editions of the event.


This project also was of personal significance to me being my first professional design project.

Ideation

Sketching

It started with the brainstorming process and analyzing inputs from the students of the organizing committee. A central input was towards shifting from wordmark logo towards an abstract or a combination based logo. The new design was expected to be more playful and fresh still simple with abstract elements.



Color Pallette

After initial sketching, I started developing a visual language to keep things consistent across all the platforms of the event. I wanted something that felt slick, energizing and modern while retaining the tradition of the event it has developed over the years.

Design

Logo

The final logo is a combination of Abstract and Lettermark Logo. The shade of green gave the feeling of freshness while the grey represented a sense of accessibility and somberness. The addition of a slight gradient made the logo more playful and modern-looking which corresponded with the event strategy.

Promotional Poster

Multiple Promotional Posters were designed to create awareness about the events on various campuses. To make them more relatable with the target audience references to the renowned personalities in the I.T. Industry and devices were used. 

Brochure

I designed a 4-fold brochure with all the details regarding the different competition and seminars in Equinox. Each individual competition or seminar had its own icon to differentiate it. This approach along with the information helped us in the overall management of the event as well.

Merchandise

To make the event engaging for the students we designed official merchandise for the event and had arranged for presale of it during event registration and other promotional activities. The merchandise included t-shirt, wristbands, event bands and badges.


The Merchandise was an instant hit and over 300+ Merchandise Set (t-shirt+band) were sold. Students in the department still wear the t-shirt with pride.

Final Takeaways

The event was a tremendous success and received a record turnout of participants for it. The design was appreciated along with promotional strategies such as the introduction of merchandise.


Being my first professional project, being true to the event's tagline this was the best platform for me to develop and test my design skills. There are many things I would have done differently especially in terms of color palette and the consistency in the overall branding project. Having said that this project will always be of personal significance for me.
